The Wanderer guide to Abu Dhabi
Visiting Abu Dhabi was a feast for the senses—everything from the music to the food to the friendly locals just added more layers to my incredible experience. Thrill-seekers, don’t fret. Prepare for some heart-stopping, jaw-dropping, and exhilarating adventures in Abu Dhabi—from F1 racing to skydiving from 13,000 feet. But let’s also sprinkle some calmer, more easygoing activities in this list for good measure, like kayaking in stunning mangroves and walking around amusement parks. I didn’t know what to expect in this beautiful city, but I am bringing back a plethora of recommendations.
